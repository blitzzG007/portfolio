About Me

My name is Aly, and I am a dedicated front-end development student with a strong interest in UI and UX design. My journey into this field began in 2024, and since then, I have continued to build my skills through self-study, personal projects, and formal education.


In August, I began my studies at Nuroff, where I am developing solid knowledge in HTML, CSS, and user-centered design principles. I actively work with Figma, creating interface designs, prototypes, and visual layouts that focus on usability and clarity. Through this, I’ve learned how design decisions directly impact the development process and the overall user experience.


As part of my training, I am also gaining experience with project methodologies such as Agile and Kanban, as well as foundational project management practices. These tools help me understand how digital products are planned, structured, and delivered in professional environments.


I am committed to growing as both a designer and developer, with the long-term goal of becoming a proficient front-end developer capable of delivering polished, accessible, and thoughtful digital solutions.
